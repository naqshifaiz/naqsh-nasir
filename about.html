About

I grew up in Pakistan, where photography started as a quiet habit long before it became anything serious: borrowing cameras, filling memory cards with everyday scenes, and slowly learning to notice the way light falls on a face. It’s been a passion for as long as I can remember.

These days I’m based in Helsinki, and portraits are what I keep coming back to. I’m drawn to the quiet, in-between moments as much as the posed ones. I am also an avid traveller so you'll see pictures from around the World in my portfolio.

I shoot digitally with a Nikon D750, and also on film when I want to slow down and work more deliberately. If any of this resonates with you, I’d love to hear from you.
